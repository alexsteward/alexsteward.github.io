Frequently Asked Questions

×

How long do repairs typically take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Simple repairs like screen replacements are often done same-day, while more complex issues may take longer. We'll always give you an estimated completion time when you drop off your device.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty that covers both parts and labor. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and cash. We also offer financing options for larger repairs through Affirm.

Do I need to make an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but appointments are recommended for faster service. You can schedule an appointment through our website or by calling us directly.

What if my device can't be repaired?

If we determine that your device cannot be repaired, you won't be charged for our diagnostic service. We can also offer advice on replacement options, including our selection of quality refurbished devices.
