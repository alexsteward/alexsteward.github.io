Our Founder: Rqsst

In early 2016, Rqsst discovered his enthusiasm for PvP on Mineplex and quickly rose to prominence by winning several major competitive tournaments. After many requests, he founded The Dojo to train and mentor players. Over the years, Rqsst helped hundreds of members develop their skills, shifting the focus as Minecraft PvP servers evolved. He eventually passed leadership to his top student, Rseu, who transitioned The Dojo from Lunar to Minemen as Minecraft’s PvP scene changed.

About The Dojo

The Dojo is a community founded in 2016 by Rqsst, focused on Minecraft PvP, specifically versions 1.7-1.8. Throughout the years, The Dojo has evolved into a platform for honing PvP skills, offering expert training, strategy sessions, and a competitive environment for players of all levels. Under Rseu's leadership, it continues to thrive, helping players engage with the PvP community and excel.

Our Mission

To build a community where PvP enthusiasts can refine their skills, engage in competitive gameplay, and grow alongside other passionate players. The Dojo stands as a training ground for individuals who are dedicated to mastering Minecraft combat mechanics.
