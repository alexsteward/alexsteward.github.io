The Dojo

Our Founder: Rqsst

Rqsst founded The Dojo in early 2016 and first discovered his enthusiasm for PvP on Mineplex. He gained recognition by winning the CCL Season 2 Playoffs hosted by MCL (Mineplex Competitive League) and later won the CCL Season 3 Playoffs and BCL Ranking tournament. After winning the BCL Tournament, he launched a training guild to help others improve. As Mineplex's popularity waned, he shifted focus to Lunar, a new flourishing PvP server. He eventually retired due to personal issues and handed the guild over to his #1 pupil, Rseu, who then shifted the focus from Lunar to Minemen after Lunar shutdown in early 2023.

About The Dojo

Founded in 2016, The Dojo has dedicated itself to PvP training and excellence. Originally focusing on Mineplex and later transitioning to Lunar PvP, we now embrace Minemen as our primary server. We exclusively use Minecraft versions 1.7 to 1.8, ensuring a competitive environment for all members. The Dojo was briefly put on hiatus in 2022, but our community remains strong and dedicated to helping new players improve.
