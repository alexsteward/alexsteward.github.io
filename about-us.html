Our Founder

Rqsst

Founded The Dojo in 2016, Rqsst has been a dedicated leader, teaching over 100 aspiring players the art of PvP. His commitment to the community and skill development was evident as he primarily utilized the Lunar PvP server to hone players' abilities.

In late 2020, Rqsst resigned due to personal conflicts, handing over the reins of The Dojo to Rseu. Following this transition, Rseu shifted the dojo's focus to Minemen.club, where the community continues to thrive.

The Dojo

Founded in 2016, The Dojo is a dedicated PvP community focused on developing players’ skills in Minecraft. Initially centered around the Lunar PvP server, the dojo has since transitioned to Minemen.club as its main platform. After a brief hiatus in 2022, The Dojo continues to embrace players who wish to master the art of PvP, utilizing Minecraft version 1.8.9 for optimal gameplay.
