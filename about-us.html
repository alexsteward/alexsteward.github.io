Welcome to The Dojo!

The Dojo was originally founded in 2016 by Rqsst but has been passed down in 2020 to Rseu, who is the current owner.

We are dedicated to perfecting our PvP skills in Minecraft, using minemen.club to officialize our practice sessions. Our main focus is on PvP training, and we operate primarily on version 1.8.9 to ensure a competitive environment.

Join us as we strive to enhance our gameplay, participate in events, and foster a community of passionate Minecraft players!
