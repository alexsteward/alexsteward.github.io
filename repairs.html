Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our repair services.

How long do repairs typically take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Simple repairs like screen replacements are often done same-day, while more complex issues may take longer. We'll always give you an estimated completion time when you drop off your device.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty that covers both parts and labor. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no additional cost. This warranty does not cover physical damage, water damage, or software issues unrelated to the original repair.

Will I lose my data during the repair process?

We take every precaution to protect your data during repairs. However, we always recommend backing up your device before bringing it in for repair, as certain procedures may require a factory reset. For devices that won't power on, we offer data recovery services to help retrieve your important files.

Do I need an appointment for repair services?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize wait times. You can book an appointment through our contact form, by phone, or via email. For urgent repairs, we offer same-day emergency service based on availability.

What happens if my device can't be repaired?

If we determine that your device cannot be repaired economically, you won't be charged for our diagnostic service. We can also provide recommendations for replacement options, including our selection of quality refurbished devices, often at significant savings compared to buying new.

Do you use original manufacturer parts?

We use high-quality OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts or premium aftermarket parts that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications. We'll always discuss the parts options with you before proceeding with repairs, including any price differences between OEM and aftermarket components.
